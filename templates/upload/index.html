About Us

The scope of this work includes the design, implementation, and evaluation of a multimodal biometric authentication system. The system combines real-time face detection and recognition using the YOLOv8 framework and fingerprint recognition utilizing CNNs(Covolutional Neural Networks).
There is not a lot of datasets available on Indian faces and we tried to work on that point by starting it from University level

The aim is to facilitate the world of security and the fact that there are various organizations that uses face and fingerprint recognition to authenticate their users. Like in various government level examinations students are identified based on their face and fingerprint but the system that they uses is not that reliable and accurate soto tackle such kind of issues we are presenting BIOAuthentica , the web based biometric authentication system based on robust latest technology and machnine learning concepts.
